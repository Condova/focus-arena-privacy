
# Privacy Policy for Focus Arena

**Last updated:** [19/12/2025]

Focus Arena (“the App”) is operated by an individual developer (“we”, “us”, “our”).
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect user information when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the App’s core functionality.

### a) Account & Authentication Data

When you create an account, we collect:

* Email address
* Display name
* Authentication credentials (handled securely via third-party providers)

Authentication methods supported:

* Email & password
* Google Sign-In
* Apple Sign-In

We do not collect profile photos; the App uses built-in avatars.

---

### b) User-Generated Data

We collect and store data you voluntarily generate while using the App, including:

* Productivity sessions
* Energy Points (EP)
* Daily, weekly, and monthly goals
* Analytics related to your productivity activity
* Social features such as challenges and comparisons

This data exists solely to provide analytics, insights, and app functionality.
**We do not sell, rent, or repurpose this data for advertising.**

---

### c) Analytics & App Performance Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect:

* App usage statistics
* Feature interactions
* Crash and performance diagnostics

This data is used only to:

* Improve app stability
* Fix bugs
* Improve user experience

We do **not** use analytics data for advertising, profiling, or ad targeting.

---

### d) Purchase Information

If you purchase a subscription:

* Transactions are processed entirely by Google Play or Apple App Store
* We receive only subscription status and entitlement information

We **do not** collect or store:

* Credit card details
* Billing addresses
* Payment credentials

RevenueCat is used solely to manage subscriptions and entitlements.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

* Provide and maintain app functionality
* Sync your data across sessions and devices
* Enable premium (VIP) features
* Display personalised productivity analytics
* Improve performance and reliability
* Prevent abuse and ensure security

We do **not** use your data for advertising purposes.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

* User data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure).
* Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.
* No system is 100% secure, but we follow industry-standard practices.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell user data.

Data is shared only with trusted third-party service providers strictly required to operate the App, including:

* Google Firebase (authentication, analytics, data storage)
* Google Play / Apple App Store (payments)
* RevenueCat (subscription management)

These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

---

## 5. International Users

The App is available globally.
Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country of residence.
By using the App, you consent to this transfer where legally permitted.

---

## 6. User Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

* Access your personal data
* Correct inaccurate data
* Delete your account and associated data
* Restrict or object to certain processing

You can delete your account **directly within the App**.
When deleted, your data is permanently removed from our systems.

We do not retain personal data after deletion.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and productivity tracking.
It is **not designed specifically for children under 13**.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
